About Our Virtual Biotech Laboratory

The Virtual Biotech Laboratory is designed to help students learn biotechnology experiments through interactive and virtual methods. This platform provides equipment principle, experiment procedures, laboratory concepts, and learning materials for educational purposes.

Our website includes various biotechnology experiments such as Staining, DNA Isolation, PCR Technique, Gel Electrophoresis and Cell Culture.

The main objective of this project is to make biotechnology learning easier, interactive, and accessible for students anywhere and anytime.
