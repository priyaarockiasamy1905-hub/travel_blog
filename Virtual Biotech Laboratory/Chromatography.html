Chromatography

Procedure

  1. Prepare the stationary phase inside a column or on a flat plate.
  2. Apply the complex liquid mixture carefully onto the stationary phase.
  3. Introduce the mobile phase (solvent) to start moving through the system.
  4. Allow the solvent to carry the sample components forward at different speeds.
  5. Separate the compounds based on how strongly they stick to each phase.
  6. Collect or detect the distinct bands of components as they emerge.
  7. Analyze the final results visually or using a digital detector.
